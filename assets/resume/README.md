# Resume Folder

Place your resume PDF in this folder.

## Required File:

**resume.pdf** - Your professional resume/CV

## Instructions:

1. Save your resume as `resume.pdf` (lowercase)
2. Place it in this folder (`assets/resume/resume.pdf`)
3. The download button on your website will automatically work

## Tips:

- Keep file size under 2MB
- Use PDF format (most compatible)
- Ensure the resume is up-to-date
- Include your contact information
- Match the skills and experience shown on your website

## Creating a Professional Resume:

If you don't have a resume yet, you can use these free tools:

- [Canva](https://canva.com) - Free resume templates
- [Resume.io](https://resume.io) - Professional resume builder
- [Overleaf](https://overleaf.com) - LaTeX resume templates
- Google Docs - Free templates available

## What to Include:

- Contact information
- Professional summary
- Work experience
- Education
- Skills (match your website's skills section)
- Projects (match your portfolio section)
- Certifications (if applicable)
- Awards/Achievements (optional)

Make sure your resume complements your portfolio website!
